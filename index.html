Concore Inc.

Established 1986

Concore Inc. is a professional and experienced concrete sawing, drilling and demolition company providing a full range of services to commercial and industrial contractors as well as custom home builders and residential customers. Contractors and businesses that would use our services include general and specialty contractors, plumbing, electrical, HVAC, fire sprinkler, and property management companies. We also specialize in tenant improvements, renovations and building remodels.

We have reliably served Southern Nevada for the past 25 years. Through our dedication to excellence and commitment to service, we have established many lasting business relationships with our customers by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance during the construction process.
